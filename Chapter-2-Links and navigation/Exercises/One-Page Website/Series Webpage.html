Plot

Loki is an American television series based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name. The series is set after the events of the film Avengers: Endgame (2019), where an alternate version of Loki created a new timeline. Loki is brought to the mysterious Time Variance Authority (TVA), a bureaucratic organization that monitors the timeline. He is given a choice: face being erased from existence due to being a "time-variant" or help fix the timeline and stop a greater threat.
